HOW TO MAKE FULL ENGLISH BREAKFAST

When someone invented the phrase "meal fit for a king," we have a sneaking suspicion they might have been referring to a full English breakfast.

With a plate overflowing with traditional English food and regional favorites, these hearty breakfasts were originally made for England's farmers to keep them fueled throughout the day of hard labor.

While nowadays it's not on as many England's breakfast tables, it's still a meal that's enjoyed throughout the country as a weekend treat.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 Bread slices
  • 2 cups Baked Beans
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 4 Chicken Sausages
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 10 Mushroom
  • 4 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 spring onions chopped

STEPS TO COOK

  1. Start by preparing the baked beans. You can use canned baked beans, But I prefer to make my own spice baked beans
  2. Slice your bread and toast them with little butter till crispy. Set aside.
  3. Take a large frying pan. Add oil. Place chicken sausages, tomatoes and mushrooms. Cook these on medium to low heat till it is well seared and cooked completely. Keep tossing them around.
  4. Once they are charred to your liking, set them aside in the pan. Make room for frying some eggs.
  5. Heat butter in the pan. Add in couple of eggs. Season with salt and pepper. Cook them as per your liking.
